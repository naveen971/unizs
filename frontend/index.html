Who We Are

Pioneering the Future of Deep Technology

UNIZS Private Limited is an innovation-driven Research & Development company dedicated to designing, developing, and commercializing cutting-edge technologies that solve real-world challenges.

Our expertise spans Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, Embedded Systems, Space Technology, IoT, Drone Technology, Smart Wearables, Industrial Automation, and Product Engineering.

Beyond product development, we empower students, researchers, startups, and industries through innovation programs, STEM education, internships, workshops, industrial training, and research collaborations.
